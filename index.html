About Our Studio

Yoga Chittagong is more than just a yoga studio; it's a community dedicated to wellness and mindfulness. Located in the vibrant Halishahar area, we offer a peaceful escape from the urban hustle.

Our certified instructors guide you through practices that strengthen the body, calm the mind, and nourish the soul, regardless of your experience level.
